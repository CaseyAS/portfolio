Hello World. My name is Casey Smith. I was born at St Vincent's hospital and raised in the Portland Metro area. I am mixed race. The easy way to say it, is that I’m half black half white. But that isn’t the whole story, each side has a kaleidoscope of races and culture. I have yet to take a DNA test to sperate them out, so I am not myself sure of my own make up.

I have one older sibling whom is a successful event coordinator, residing in the bay area of California and my mother lives close to me in Beaverton Oregon. I also have extended family in south in Keizer Oregon and other states, such as Montana and North Carolina.
